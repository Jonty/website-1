Who
===

We have two Lightning Talk proposals so far, but you can also turn up
on the night and talk.

Subject: DEF CON 22, CTF finals, the game & some of the cool challenges

Subject: Introduction to SCADA, how computer controlled industiral systems have evolved.
Speaker: Dom Cashley


The qualifications for speakers are simple, have a subject of interest
to fellow technical & InfoSec people. You can be an expert, a student
or someone learning a new area, maybe a regular speaker on the
conference circuit, but we are also very welcoming to new speakers,
and their often new ideas or perspectives.
